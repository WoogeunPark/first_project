QR Code Types: Explanations and Examples

Text / URL

Description: Creates a QR code that contains plain text or a web link. When a URL is scanned, it will open the website in a browser.

Usage Example: Use on posters, business cards, or product packaging to direct users to your website, a social media profile, or a promotional landing page.

Wi-Fi

Description: Allows users to connect to a Wi-Fi network automatically without manually entering the SSID and password.

Usage Example: Perfect for cafes, offices, or homes. Display the QR code so guests can connect to your Wi-Fi instantly and securely.

Email

Description: Generates a QR code that opens the user's default email client with a pre-filled recipient, subject, and message body.

Usage Example: Place on a contact page or business card to let potential clients or customers email you with a single scan.

SMS

Description: Creates a QR code that opens the user's messaging app with a pre-filled phone number and message.

Usage Example: Use in marketing campaigns where you want users to text a specific code or message, for example, to subscribe to a service or enter a contest.

vCard

Description: A digital business card. Scanning it allows users to save your contact information (name, phone, email) directly to their phone's address book.

Usage Example: The ultimate networking tool. Put this on your business card or in your email signature to make it effortless for others to save your contact details.
